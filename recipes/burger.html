Ingredients:

  • Buns
  • Beef patties
  • Pickles

Steps:

  1. Stir fry onions in pan.
  2. Add beef patty on top of onions.
  3. Flip patty then add cheese on top.
  4. Toast buns in butter.
  5. Assemble burger and add sauce to liking.
